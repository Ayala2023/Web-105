Camping in Arkansas

Devils Lake

Details 1

The next park that me and my family went to was Crater Diamond State Park in Pike County, Arkansas. I’ve gone with my family twice on this camp out.

I had a fun and interesting experience there. Basically, the park had a big landscape that was basically all dirt. Imagine yourself you’re in a corn field, with nothing there, just dirt on the ground. Anyway, the purpose of that place is for people to search diamonds or any other minerals that people can find.

Details 2

The park provided tools and stations where you can clean the dirt that you picked up on the ground and wash it with water.I remember one time, a mom and her older son were next to us finding diamonds, when suddenly the mom told her son to look closer at a shiny red mineral that she found, and later the son confirmed it was a red ruby; amazing of what you can find in this park.

Details 3

I remember one time during the night, as my siblings were sleeping, we could here the cayotes howling, but at a distance. I remember eating pot pie for the first time at the camp site, super good, 9.5/10 rating, including the camp out.

"If I ever found a diamond, I will cash it in, and I would buy cool presents for my family, that would be so cool!"

Alain Ayala
