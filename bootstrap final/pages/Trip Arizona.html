Camping in Arizona

Grand Canyon

Desciption 1

The last park that I’m going to talk about that me and my family went to is the Grand Canyon National Park. This park has many animals and different plants there living. Now lets move on to the final park.

The campgrounds and the park were cool and interesting. Many animals that I’ve never seen with my own eyes. I remember pulling up to the park, my family and I saw a big elk the was taller than Shaq, but way wider. It looks like it weighs more than 300 pounds.

Description 2

Funny fact, my family and I got to experience more of these creatures throughout the camp site. When my family and I head to the Grand Canyon itself, we experience some squirrels that were close than ever.Never in my life have I seen squirrels come up close and asking for some food.

Description 3

One time me and my family went to a nigh sky show, where you get to see the stars brighter in the sky, and as I was watching it, the stars felt really close to my face, it was hard to look away, something I’ve never experienced before. I would rate this camp out an 8/10.

"Okay, this time, I will actually see how the sun goes down, but I also want to know how fast does it go down?"

Alain Ayala
