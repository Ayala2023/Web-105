Camping in Colorado

Mesa Verde

Desciption 1

The next park that me and my family went to was National Park Mesa Verda Colorado. Really had some great memories and interesting experience during this campout.

My family and I saw old building structures that the Native-Americans built, that was cool.
Every time it was sunset in that campground, it felt warm and nice, probably because we were close to the west side of the country, so that makes sense.

Description 2

This campout was fun, me and my family were experiencing many different site views that we have never seen in our lives. I forgot to describe the reason why you see me and my siblings wearing the same shirt, the reason for that is because my aunt thought it was a good idea to have the same matching shirts for all the kids, for if we get lost somwehere and another family finds us, they can tell which family we belong to.
