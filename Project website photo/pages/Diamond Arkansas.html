Arkansas Park Trip

The next park that me and my family went to was Crater Diamond State Park in Pike County, Arkansas. I’ve gone with my family twice on this camp out.

More Photos

I had a fun and interesting experience there. Basically, the park had a big landscape that was basically all dirt. Imagine yourself you’re in a corn field, with nothing there, just dirt on the ground. Anyway, the purpose of that place is for people to search diamonds or any other minerals that people can find.


The park provided tools and stations where you
can clean the dirt that you picked up on the ground and wash it with water.
I remember one time, a mom and her older son
were next to us finding diamonds, when suddenly
the mom told her son to look closer at a shiny red mineral
that she found, and later the son confirmed it was
a red ruby; amazing of what you can find in this park.
